About Us



Based On Acts 2

"To come together in one accord, To Repent, To receive conviction, To be baptized, To be devoted to his word, To have fellowship with one another and worship god in unity"


History
Haven To Rest Ministry was founded by Pastor Justin Naidoo and his wife Shelia. A church where people can recieve the word of God and be renewed in the spirit.



Our Vision
Our vision is to embrace, encourage, evangelise, equip, experience and extend.

Mission
Haven to rest is on a church organisation, which on a mission evangelise to the world and preach the gospel of our lord and saviour Jesus Christ, we accomplish this by embracing people our church Encouraging people to seek God and welcome him into our lives. Equiping people with the word of God so they persevere through trials and have experience for the other trails down the road. Last but not least we aim to extend the kingdom of God.
